## Billing Views

Admins/Owners see a toggle on the billing page:

| View | Shows |
|------|-------|
| **Organization** | Master wallet, org-wide usage, all transactions |
| **Personal** | Individual quota, personal usage |

Members only see their personal quota.

## Wallet Structure

- **Organization Quota:** Main budget. Sessions deduct from here.
- **Member Quota:** Per-user allocation from org budget.
- **Personal:** Separate wallet if user has personal account outside org.

### What You Can Track

- **Scenario overview:** Sessions, duration, avg length, creation date
- **Usage over time:** Visualizations and trends
- **Top scenarios:** Most popular content
- **Date filtering:** Last 7/30 days, quarter, all time, custom

### Exporting Data

| Export | Contains | Best for |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Scenarios CSV** | Scenario-level stats | Executive reports |
| **Sessions CSV** | User, timestamps, duration, scores | LMS import, ROI |

### L&D Insights

- **Engagement:** Which scenarios are actually used?
- **Completion rates:** Low = too long or not engaging
- **Time investment:** Justify training costs
- **Individual progress:** Who's keeping up in cohort programs?
