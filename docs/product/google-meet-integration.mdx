## Two Ways to Use Meeting Bot

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Custom Meeting Mode">
    **Best for:** One-off meetings, dual meetings, or ad-hoc meetings
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>Provide the meeting URL or Google Meet link</Step>
      <Step>Specify when you want the bot to join</Step>
      <Step>Done! The bot will join at the specified time</Step>
    </Steps>
    
    Perfect for meetings that just appeared on your calendar or when you need precise control.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Calendar Integration Mode">
    **Best for:** Recurring meetings or automated joining
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>Grant access to your calendar</Step>
      <Step>Optionally specify a keyword filter (e.g., "interview 101")</Step>
      <Step>The bot automatically joins matching meetings</Step>
    </Steps>
    
    The keyword filter prevents the agent from joining meetings where you don't want it present.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is this feature available to everyone?">
    Currently, we're allowing selected users to test this feature. To get access:
    
    1. Click the meeting bot icon in your dashboard
    2. Send us a message through the platform
    
    Or email the ID you use on ToughTongue AI to **help@getarchieai.com**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I remove the bot from a Google Meet call?">
    The Google Meet admin (person who sent the invite) can remove the bot from the meeting. This is currently the only way to end the bot's participation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens to the analysis after the bot is removed?">
    The session will be recorded and processed normally for analysis. The user name recorded for analysis is Google Meet's meeting title.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple calendars?">
    Yes, you can connect and manage multiple calendars through the integration.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does this feature cost?">
    **Through August 2025:** Free for all usersâ€”you only consume your existing minutes available on the ToughTongue AI platform.
    
    **After August 2025:** Available for:
    - Premium subscribers ($20/month or $200/year plans onlyâ€”not available in Free or Standard $12/month plans)
    - Business plan users ($99 and above)
    
    For business deals and custom pricing, email **help@getarchieai.com**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I grant calendar access?">
    We're currently getting our calendar access reviewed by Google. You may see a warning that says the app is "unsafe," but you can proceed by granting the necessary permissions to use this feature.
    
    **Alternative:** If you prefer not to grant calendar access, you can use the Custom Meeting mode insteadâ€”simply provide the meeting URL directly without needing any calendar integration.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Which meeting platforms are supported?">
    Currently, we support **Google Meet** with **Zoom** and **Microsoft Teams** coming soon.
    
    Feel free to reach out with a request at **help@getarchieai.com**
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does the bot introduce itself when joining?">
    The bot will introduce itself based on your scenario settings. You can customize the introduction in your scenario configuration.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Does the bot record meetings and perform analysis?">
    Yes! The bot automatically:
    - Generates transcripts
    - Performs analysis (based on settings in your scenario: rubric, auto analysis option, etc.)
    - Stores session data for review
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What are the current limitations?">
    1. **Background noise sensitivity:** Works best in quiet environments
    2. **Multiple speakers:** While multiple people can be in the meeting, the agent performs best when one person speaks at a time. If multiple people speak simultaneously, the agent can get confused about who to respond to
    3. **Platform support:** Currently limited to Google Meet (more platforms coming soon)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Details

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How It Works">
    1. **Meeting Detection:** Based on calendar or custom URL
    2. **Bot Joins:** At specified time, AI agent enters the meeting
    3. **Audio Processing:** Real-time transcription and understanding
    4. **AI Response:** Natural conversation with sub-second latency
    5. **Recording & Analysis:** Transcript and insights saved to your dashboard
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Privacy & Security">
    - All recordings are encrypted at rest and in transit
    - Only you and authorized org members can access transcripts
    - Compliant with GDPR, SOC 2, and enterprise security standards
    - Participants should be informed of recording (your responsibility)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Audio Quality">
    **Requirements:**
    - Stable internet connection (10+ Mbps recommended)
    - Quiet environment preferred
    - One primary speaker at a time
    
    **Supported:**
    - Multiple participants (AI responds to active speaker)
    - Background noise filtering (best-effort)
    - International accents
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
